Q. How do we know that the surveyor approaching ia a true government official?
A. The government has provided each surveyor with an ID-card, please tally the photograph as well as other details. Moreover he/she has a unique surveyor ID to login at the website. All the necessary details of the surveyor are displayed on the screen after login.

Q. What if we are mistaken to submit wrong details? How to get them corrected?
A. After submitting your details, a link of your data will be sent to you via SMS. You can correct your errors there, within a time limit of 10 days. The link expires as the time limit expires.
